Google has decided to replace its search box with an LLM chat box this week, 
a decision that can only be called "bad" for [public information](https://futurism.com/artificial-intelligence/google-ai-overviews-misinformation), 
[public health](https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2026/jan/02/google-ai-overviews-risk-harm-misleading-health-information),
accessibility to information.

This is a
good opportunity to [switch your search engine in
Firefox](https://superuser.com/a/1756774) to `noai.duckduckgo.com/?q=%s` to get
regular boring search results without an AI overview. If you also install ublock origin and
add the [AI
blocklist](https://github.com/laylavish/uBlockOrigin-HUGE-AI-Blocklist/tree/main),
it does a pretty good job of removing slop sites like deepwiki[^1] from the
search results too. ublock conveniently gives you the ability to ignore cookie
nags and social widgets by checking those boxes in settings, recommend. 
If you don't take it from me, [take it
from Taylor
Swift](https://xcancel.com/SwiftOnSecurity/status/1848539576102539686#m),
installing uBlock is one of the best things you can do to reduce your exposure
to malware. 

[^1]: The garbage gitub repository summariser that's an insult to anyone who's ever written code documentation.
