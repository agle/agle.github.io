I am alicia, a static program analysis research engineer from Meanjin (Brisbane), Australia.

I work within UQ-PAC, at the University of Queensland, mainly working on binary analysis for program verification.

I have Bachelors’ degrees in Mathematics and Computer Science from the University of Queensland.

My interests are static analysis, compilers, and automated reasoning and verification. I've also been a large fan of OCaml lately.
